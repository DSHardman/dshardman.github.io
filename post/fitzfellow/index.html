Henslow Fellowship Election

David Hardman
Oct 17, 2024 1 min read

From October 2024, I’ve started a new position as a Henslow Fellow at Fitzwilliam College, Cambridge. This is a 3-year Junior Research Fellowship supported by Cambridge Philosophical Society, a society with a fascinating involvement and history of shaping modern science.

I’m very excited to dive into this role; I have some very fond memories of the past 8 years at Corpus, but I’m now able to mark my transition from student life by becoming just as involved with the Fitz community!
